Micro lessons

Micro Lessons for Classroom Teaching and Self‑Study education revolution

These lessons are part of my ongoing work as a test designer, UX learning designer, and EdTech specialist. They showcase the kind of interactive, research-based materials I create—fast, focused activities that build real exam skills as well as everyday communication skills.

Each lesson is fully responsive and can be used on a computer, phone, tablet, or projected onto a classroom whiteboard. Teachers can use them as warm-ups, extension activities, or quick feedback tools. Learners can use them independently at home to strengthen areas that textbooks and traditional curricula often miss.

The lessons target pronunciation, listening, micro-writing, grammar accuracy, and common learner errors across IELTS, PTE, CAE, and general English. They are short, practical, and designed to fill the gaps that matter— supporting both test preparation and clearer, more confident communication in an English-speaking environment.
